# What is an AI-Native Database? — 30-Second Animation Script

**Duration:** 30 seconds
**Goal:** Explain what an AI-native database is and why we need one.
**Style:** Dark, clean motion graphics. Punchy, conceptual.
**Color Palette:** Background `#0A0A0F`, Blue `#3B82F6`, Amber `#F59E0B`, Green `#10B981`, Red `#EF4444`

---

## Scene 1 — Databases Were Built for Humans (0:00 – 0:08)

**Visual:** A timeline showing the evolution of databases. Icons appear left to right: filing cabinet (1970s relational), document icon (2000s NoSQL), magnifying glass over vectors (2020s vector DB). Each was designed for a human typing queries.

A human figure sits at a keyboard, typing precise queries. Results come back. It works.

**Text on screen:** "Databases were built for humans."

**Narration:**
> "For fifty years, databases were built for humans — people who know exactly what to search for and where to look."

---

## Scene 2 — Now AI Agents Need Data Too (0:08 – 0:16)

**Visual:** The human figure slides aside. An AI agent icon takes its place. It needs to answer a messy, open-ended question: *"What are our contractual obligations if a vendor misses a delivery deadline?"*

The AI tries to query a traditional database / vector DB. It gets back random chunks — fragments with no structure, no hierarchy, no context. The AI assembles a broken, uncertain answer. Red glow. Confidence: low.

**Text on screen:** "But AI agents don't search. They need to reason."

**Narration:**
> "But now AI agents need to answer complex questions — and today's databases give them disconnected fragments with no structure. They can search, but they can't reason."

---

## Scene 3 — What an AI-Native Database Looks Like (0:16 – 0:25)

**Visual:** The fragments dissolve. A new kind of database appears — documents stored as glowing, navigable trees. Root nodes hold summaries. Branches lead to sections. Leaves hold the actual content.

The AI agent enters the tree. It reads the root summary, picks the right branch ("Delivery Obligations"), drills one level deeper ("Missed Deadline Penalties"), and lands on the precise clause. Green glow. The answer assembles with a clear source trail: *Root → Section 4 → 4.2*.

**Text on screen:** "An AI-native database lets agents reason through documents — not just retrieve chunks."

**Narration:**
> "An AI-native database stores documents as knowledge trees — so agents can navigate structure, read summaries, and drill to the precise answer."

---

## Scene 4 — Close (0:25 – 0:30)

**Visual:** The tree pulls back. Text appears centered, clean and bold.

**Text on screen:**
- "ReasonDB"
- "AI-Native Document Intelligence"
- "Built for agents that reason, not just retrieve."

**Narration:**
> "ReasonDB. Built for AI agents that reason, not just retrieve."

## STYLE NOTES FOR VFX TEAM

- **The arc:** Human era → AI era → the gap → the solution. Clean narrative.
- **Scene 1** should feel nostalgic/established — these are the databases we know.
- **Scene 2** is the tension — the AI is struggling. Make the fragments feel chaotic and wrong.
- **Scene 3** is the payoff — the tree should feel elegant, alive, intentional. The AI navigating it should feel smooth and confident, like it finally has the right tool.
- **The tree** is the single most important visual. It should be beautiful and memorable.
- **No logo** — the name "ReasonDB" only appears as text in the final 5 seconds.
- **Typography:** Inter Bold for overlays, large and readable. Minimal words on screen.
- **Sound:** Ambient synth, a "whoosh" when the AI enters the tree, a satisfying "chime" on the answer.
